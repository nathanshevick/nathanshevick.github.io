A Great Spice That Tastes Nice

Recently I created a new breakfast item that I enjoy a lot! The base is a mini croissant cut in half, then on top of it nutella and sliced bananas. Now I enjoyed this a lot and was very happy with my creation. Nevertheless, it was missing SOMETHING . I thought and thought of what I could change to make my dish the best it couold be. Not another main flavor that would overpower the choclate hazelnut spread or sweet bananas. It needed a compliment to make it taste better but not take anything away from what was there. Then it hit me. Cinnamon Sugar.
